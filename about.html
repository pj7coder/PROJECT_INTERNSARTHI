About InternSarthi

InternSarthi is a smart internship recommendation platform designed to guide students towards the best opportunities that match their skills, interests, and career goals. We understand that finding the right internship can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available online. That’s why InternSarthi simplifies the process by curating internships that are relevant, verified, and tailored to each student’s profile.

Our platform uses a recommendation system that considers your academic background, skills, and preferences to suggest internships that align with your career path. Whether you are a beginner looking for your first industry exposure or an advanced learner seeking specialized projects, InternSarthi provides opportunities suited for all levels.

At InternSarthi, we believe internships are not just about work experience—they are stepping stones to building confidence, learning practical skills, and discovering your true potential. Our mission is to connect students with the right opportunities and help them grow into future-ready professionals.
